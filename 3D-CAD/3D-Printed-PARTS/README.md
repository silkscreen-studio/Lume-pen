# 3D printed parts

The Lume body is made of 3 Parts that can be printed via JLC 3DP services. 2 MJF Nylon parts, and one clear resin part.
The charger cover can be printed in Nylon too, to get a premium surface finish, while a few other parts, more functionnal than aesthetic, could be 3D printed with a .2mm nozzle (FDM).

The 3 pen body parts would then be glued together once the pen is fully assembled, and for the charger, resin will be poured around the FPC inside the charger cover, with on top the charger plate, to provide support for the pogopins, while also creating a diffusing window for the 6 LEDs.
